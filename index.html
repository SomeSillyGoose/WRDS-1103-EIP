Scroll-triggered topic cards

Scrolling changes how quickly information is processed. When content is revealed in stages, readers focus on a single idea before moving to the next one.

Use this card to introduce the main point. The square title block can sit on either side and expand into a fuller explanation when clicked.

Alternating layout

Alternating the square title block from left to right makes each section feel distinct while keeping the visual system consistent.

The extension panel animates open beside the main card, creating a clear sense of progression instead of dumping all of the text on screen at once.
